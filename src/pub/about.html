Background

OpenCM was originally developed in 2017 to support governance and control over installed and configured webMethods components. The intention is to provide a means for documenting the required configuration properties as well as an ability to audit properties that are used within runtime installations.

Over the course of implementing such a tool, it also became apparent that an inventory of servers and installations are properly defined, along with endpoint information. This is also a prerequisite for being able to inspect/retrieve configuration information from remotely installed runtimes. Focus has therefore also been to easily manage such an "inventory" of integration platform components.
